Welcome to Ben's Bombastic Bakery home to the most delictable baked goods in all of Ireland! We pride ourself on satifying our customers and building a strong relationship within our community

On this site you can order our freshly baked treats straight to your doorstep or you can come visit where the magic happens.

We are located on 7 Bakewell Street in Mallow, County Cork

Opening Hours

Our opening hours are:

  • Monday - Friday: 9am - 7pm
  • Saturday: 9am - 4pm
  • Sunday: Closed

How to Order

To order our delicious treats to your doorstep you must:

  1. Create an account or login to your account
  2. Visit our menu page
  3. Select the items you want and how much of each item you would like
  4. Specify if you want it delivered or pick-up in store, If it is delivery you must:
    • Enter your address and postcode
    • Enter your phone number
  5. Confirm your order and wait for the goods to come to you!
